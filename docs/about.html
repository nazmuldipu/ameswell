Dedicated to Sustainability

The Ameswell Hotel incorporates sustainable products and practices throughout the property and in our daily operations.

We are committed to offering the highest quality, locally-sourced, organic products while constantly seeking new ways to reduce our impact on Mountain View’s vital natural resources.

Our sustainability program includes:

  • Biodegradable or reusable containers at all restaurants
  • In-room recycling options
  • 100% organic bath amenities by Juice Beauty
  • Electric car charging stations
  • Partnerships with farmers/ranchers
  • Almost no single-use plastic

Who are we?

We are sustainable

We are the greenest hotel in Silicon Valley

From the solar panels powering our LED bulbs to our connection to the local bike and hiking trail. We have eliminated 96% of single-use plastic on premises – and won’t stop until we’re at 100%. Enjoy organic bath products and locally sourced food knowing we’re on the same page: sustainability first.

We are relaxed luxury

We offer a new kind of approachable sophistication

Beautifully designed, yet unfussy; high-tech and still high-touch. We have just what you need (and nothing you don’t) – excess has been edited out.

We are about wellbeing

At Ameswell, health means mind, body, and soul

From our medical-grade air filtration to contactless ordering systems, safety is paramount. Enjoy a meditation session or workout on The Mirror. Stretch out at the pool or in our fitness center. Hop on a bike trail – real or via Peloton. The Ameswell will keep you grounded so you’re ready for liftoff.

We are rooted in community

The Ameswell is a home base for all walks of life

Whether you’re from across the planet, across the country or across the street. We’re a place to solidify connections and build something stronger and more fun, together.

We are always innovating

The Ameswell mirrors the innovation that happens daily here in Silicon Valley

We’re never satisfied with the status quo. We are inspired by our neighbors at NASA’s Ames Research Center, who pioneered jet propulsion – our goal is to keep experimenting and push the frontiers of amazing hospitality.

We are independent

We stand out from the crowd, just like the people who stay with us

We are made for adventurers and self-starters; those who blaze their own trails. As an independent brand, we’re accountable to one opinion - yours.

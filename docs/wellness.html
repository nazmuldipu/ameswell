Focused on your wellness

Whether you are here for work travel or weekend getaways, we are committed to your health and well-being, first and foremost. Starting with the design of the hotel itself—sustainable, with medical-grade filtered air throughout the guest rooms and public spaces, water filtration, superior acoustics, and human-centered private and public spaces. Continuing through your full experience both indoors and outdoors—reading in the library, relaxing at the spa, picnicking on the lawn, biking the Stevens Creek Trail.

Take a Deep Breath...

At The Ameswell Hotel, we understand that the quality of air we breathe affects our health, comfort, and well-being. Our property was designed to provide the best air quality for our guests. Air throughout The Ameswell Hotel is continuously circulated with a Dedicated Outside Air System (DOAS), which runs 24/7, constantly providing fresh, filtered air.
